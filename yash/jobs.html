Jobs In Digital Marketing

Hover over each card to discover exciting career opportunities

01
Photographer

Photographer

National average salary: ₹ 2,73,213 per year

Photographers are professionals that are skilled in capturing images using cameras and other photographic equipment. Their expertise is essential for digital content creation with the widespread use of high-quality digital images in e-commerce, advertising and web design. Many photographers who work in digital marketing specialise in niche areas of photography including product photography and the creation of photographs specifically for blogs or social media.

02
Graphic Designer

Graphic Designer

National average salary: ₹ 2,80,524 per year

Graphic designers are skilled in the technical production of visual content that includes illustrations, visual effects and typography. They can produce their work using software-based design and drawing programmes. Digital marketing agencies and businesses can use the graphic elements they produce digitally in websites, branding, digital advertisements, social media posts, email marketing campaigns and mobile apps.

03
SEO Specialist

SEO Specialist

National average salary: ₹ 3,07,201 per year

Search engine optimisation (SEO) specialists use a variety of techniques to increase the visibility of websites on online search engines. By improving the rank of a website in search engine results pages (SERPs), an SEO specialist can increase the amount of traffic and conversions the website achieves. SEO specialists undertake detailed keyword research and reviews of analytical data for the website to create detailed strategies for improving the performance of the website online.

04
Social Media Specialist

Social Media Specialist

National average salary: ₹ 3,08,842 per year

Social media specialists manage the social media accounts of the clients of a digital marketing agency. They usually have specialist knowledge of the algorithms of the leading social media platforms and create content that is carefully tailored to attract and keep a target audience. Other job responsibilities of a social media specialist include creating advertisements, tracking analytics and drafting reports for clients so they can follow the progress of their social media marketing strategy.

05
Videographer

Videographer

National average salary: ₹ 3,50,602 per year

Videographers are creative professionals who record, edit and publish video content using cameras and even smartphones. They film events, dramas, choreographed routines or promotional materials for digital marketing campaigns. They work closely with creative directors, social media specialists, graphic designers and other marketing professionals to create videos for video streaming platforms, websites and social media.

06
Digital Marketer

Digital Marketer

National average salary: ₹ 3,34,436 per year

Digital marketers are versatile marketing professionals who implement a digital marketing strategy across all online channels for an organisation. The strategies they create span long-term campaigns that build brand awareness and audience engagement and short-term activities like advertising or promotions. A digital marketer works closely with other digital marketing professionals and uses a range of digital analytical tools to monitor website traffic and track purchase behaviours.

07
Marketing Specialist

Marketing Specialist

National average salary: ₹ 3,29,262 per year

Marketing specialists are professionals who have skills in specific areas of marketing like email marketing or content marketing. They usually focus on generating leads and sales using either inbound or outbound marketing techniques on the digital channels their employer uses. Like other digital marketing professionals, they have proficiency in a wide range of analytical tools to help them optimise their campaigns.

08
Copywriter

Copywriter

National average salary: ₹ 4,13,919 per year

Companies employ copywriters to produce a wide range of written digital marketing material including, blogs, social media posts, websites and emails. Their work often involves careful market research to identify the type of language and keywords that are effective at growing and engaging an audience or selling products and services. They often collaborate with advertising specialists, web developers, photographers and graphic designers to create captivating and persuasive marketing campaigns.

09
Influencer

Influencer

National average salary: ₹ 4,69,894 per year

Influencers have expertise in accruing large numbers of online followers on various social media platforms. They then form strategic partnerships with companies and brands that use them to promote a variety of products and services in return for commissions and fees. Influencers are skilled in the production of photos, videos and written content that showcases products and services their followers may be interested in purchasing.

10
User Experience Designer

User Experience Designer

National average salary: ₹ 5,66,922 per year

User experience or UX designers are web developers who focus on optimising and enhancing the interactions and experience of users with websites, software and applications. They also troubleshoot issues and assess existing digital products by conducting usability tests, and performing in-depth market research and competitor analysis. They work closely with digital marketing professionals including web designers, graphic designers and creative directors to make meaningful changes that increase a company's website performance and sales.

11
Content Strategist

Content Strategist

National average salary: ₹ 4,69,309 per year

A content strategist develops a cohesive strategy for the creation of engaging visual and written content for an organisation. They undertake detailed market research that guides the creation of videos, photoshoots, blog posts and graphics, matching the tone, style and aesthetic of the brand. The aim of a content strategist is to consistently publish content that improves brand loyalty and increases sales.

12
E-Commerce Manager

E-Commerce Manager

National average salary: ₹ 5,44,783 per year

E-commerce managers have oversight of the operations of an e-commerce business and the website in particular. E-commerce managers collect data and analyse website performance to identify opportunities for commercial improvement and collaborate with other digital marketing professionals to implement their plans. They optimise the features, design and format of e-commerce stores, making it easier for customers to use and buy from the website.

13
Front-End Developer

Front-End Developer

National average salary: ₹ 5,88,722 per year

A front-end developer creates the areas of a website or mobile application that are for users. They alter the look and feel of a website using various programming languages and monitor website performance to identify and troubleshoot potential issues related to site usability. Front-end developers work alongside back-end developers, graphic designers, copywriters and digital marketing specialists to track analytics and implement routine upgrades as required by their employer.

14
PPC Manager

PPC Manager

National average salary: ₹ 5,87,621 per year

A PPC manager is a pay-per-click advertising specialist who manages the paid advertising campaigns of companies. They lead a team of professionals who create advertisements that occupy premium positions in relevant online searches on the leading search engines. They have expertise in bidding for online advertising space, optimising pay-per-click campaigns and keeping the ad spend within budget so that their employer makes a profit.

15
Project Manager

Project Manager

National average salary: ₹ 7,52,910 per year

Project managers are professionals who have oversight of large digital marketing projects like e-commerce or website development. They are adept at structuring the project so that it meets the client's timescale and budgetary requirements with the delegation of tasks to the creative professionals that undertake the work. Project managers are also skilled communicators who are usually the main point of contact between the project stakeholders and produce charts and reports that keep them updated on the progress of the work.

16
Creative Director

Creative Director

National average salary: ₹ 8,74,694 per year

A creative director develops and maintains the brand image of a company, organisation or publication. Their work involves creating layouts for digital publications, developing visual guidelines for social media and authoring brand manuals that ensure that marketers and creative professionals keep the brand consistent and harmonised in every marketing channel they use. Creative directors undertake a meticulous review of photographs, graphics, designs and written content before approving them.
